Chain Rule


Direct Proportion : Any two quantities are said to be directly proportional, if on the increase of one quantity, the other quantity increases and vice-versa.

Example :
Work done is directly proportional to the number of men working on it.
(more men (↑), more work (↑))

Indirect Proportion : Any two quantities are said to be indirectly proportional, if on the increase of one quantity, the other quantity decreases and vice-versa.

Example :
Time taken to finish a work is inversely proportional to the number of persons on it.
(more persons (↑), less is the time taken to finish a work (↓))
